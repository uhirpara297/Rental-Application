About Us

Getting to know the heart behind the style.

Our Story

A passion for tradition, a commitment to elegance.

"Rental Application" started as a small dream to make luxury ethnic wear accessible to everyone. We believe that every individual deserves to look their absolute best on special occasions without the burden of expensive purchases that are worn only once.

Our curated collection features handpicked cholis and lehengas from across the country, ensuring that you find the perfect blend of tradition and modern style.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the traditional wear market by providing high-quality, affordable rental options, promoting sustainable fashion choices while celebrating cultural heritage.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for ethnic wear rentals, where fashion meets accessibility, and every customer feels like royalty on their special day.
